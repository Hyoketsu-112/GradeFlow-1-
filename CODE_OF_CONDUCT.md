# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in GradeFlow and its community will experience a respectful environment free from harassment and discrimination.

---

## Expected Behavior

Everyone in the GradeFlow community is expected to:

✅ **Be Respectful**

- Treat all community members with respect
- Value diverse opinions and viewpoints
- Listen actively to others
- Be open to constructive criticism

✅ **Be Inclusive**

- Welcome people of all backgrounds
- Ensure marginalized voices are heard
- Recognize and challenge biases
- Use inclusive language

✅ **Be Professional**

- Keep discussions focused and on-topic
- Disagree diplomatically
- Avoid personal attacks
- Focus on ideas, not individuals

✅ **Be Collaborative**

- Help others when you can
- Share knowledge generously
- Give credit where due
- Work towards common goals

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are **NOT tolerated**:

❌ **Harassment**

- Unwelcome attention or contact
- Intimidation or threats
- Sustained disruption
- Exclusion based on identity

❌ **Discrimination**

- Based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexuality, disability
- Age, national origin, religion, or any protected characteristic
- Direct or indirect discrimination

❌ **Abuse**

- Insults or derogatory comments
- Aggressive language or tone
- Doxing (sharing private information)
- Impersonation

❌ **Spam & Trolling**

- Off-topic messages
- Repetitive or disruptive behavior
- Intentionally inflammatory posts
- Self-promotion without value

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Email communications
- Community forums and discussions
- In-person events and meetups
- Any official GradeFlow channels

This applies to everyone: contributors, maintainers, users, and representatives.

---

## Reporting & Enforcement

### Reporting a Violation

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Privately report to**: oshinayadamilola3@gmail.com
   - Include details of the incident
   - Provide context and evidence if possible
   - Let us know if you need support

2. **What happens next**:
   - We will investigate promptly (within 48 hours)
   - We will maintain confidentiality
   - We will communicate our findings
   - We will take appropriate action

### Retaliation is Prohibited

Retaliation against anyone for reporting a violation is strictly forbidden and will result in serious consequences.

---

## Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in:

### Warning

- First-time minor violations
- Private feedback on behavior
- Opportunity to correct

### Temporary Ban

- Repeated violations
- Serious single offenses
- 1 week - 3 months from community

### Permanent Ban

- Egregious harassment or abuse
- Multiple escalated incidents
- Threats or dangerous behavior

---

## Three Strikes Policy

For escalating violations:

**Strike 1**: Warning + 24-hour cooling off period

**Strike 2**: 1-week temporary ban + mandatory reflection

**Strike 3**: Permanent ban from community

_Note: Serious violations may skip to strike 3 directly._

---

## Conflict Resolution

We believe in restorative justice where possible:

1. **Understand**: We listen to both perspectives
2. **Educate**: Help violators understand impact
3. **Repair**: Provide opportunity to make amends
4. **Restore**: Reintegrate if behavior changes

If harmful behavior continues, we escalate to suspension/ban.

---

## Special Considerations

### Mental Health & Accessibility

- We recognize stress and mental health challenges
- We encourage self-care and breaks
- Accommodations available for neurodivergent members
- Support resources available

### Power Dynamics

- Maintainers held to higher standard
- Extra vigilance against abuse of authority
- Faster intervention for maintainer misconduct

### Marginalized Communities

- Extra support for underrepresented groups
- Fast-track reporting for targeted harassment
- Community advocate available

✅ Model the behavior we expect
